**No assumptions from expectation.**
Do not presume cards are pristine or centered. Measure and verify all claims.

TASK 1 ‚Äì IMAGE QUALITY ASSESSMENT
==================================

**Interpretation logic:**

**Resolution ‚âà information density.** Ask: Can I read the smallest printed text?

**Lighting ‚âà illumination uniformity.** Ask: Are shadows directional or diffuse?

**Angle ‚âà perspective deviation.** Ask: Do vertical edges converge?

**Glare detection now uses reflectivity persistence test:**
- If a bright line maintains shape across edges ‚Üí crease.
- If it changes with lighting zone ‚Üí glare.

**Add explicit reasoning step:**
"When glare and defect coexist, mark both but label the source of confidence (e.g., 'crease confirmed by consistent shadow ridge')."

Score each factor 0-10:

**Resolution (0-10)**
- 10: Crystal clear, can read smallest text
- 7-9: Clear, minor pixelation
- 4-6: Moderate blur, some text unreadable
- 0-3: Severe blur, card details unclear

**Lighting (0-10)**
- 10: Perfect even lighting, no shadows
- 7-9: Good lighting, minor shadows
- 4-6: Uneven lighting, significant shadows
- 0-3: Poor lighting, major dark areas

**Angle (0-10)**
- 10: Perfectly straight-on view
- 7-9: Slight angle, minimal distortion
- 4-6: Noticeable angle, some distortion
- 0-3: Extreme angle, severe distortion

**Clarity (0-10)**
- 10: Sharp edges, no motion blur
- 7-9: Mostly sharp, slight blur
- 4-6: Moderate blur, details soft
- 0-3: Severe blur, details lost

**Glare Present**: true/false
- Look for bright white reflections obscuring card details

**Overall Score**: Average of all factors

**Confidence Tier**:
- "high": overall_score ‚â• 7, all corners visible
- "medium": overall_score 5-6.9, or partial glare
- "low": overall_score < 5, or corners obscured

**Fatal flag logic now includes:**
- overall_score < 6.0
- corners not visible
- or "blur > moderate" in any region

If fatal flags present:
- Add to fatal_flags array: ["severe_blur", "missing_corners", "extreme_glare"]
- Set confidence_tier = "low"

TASK 4 ‚Äì DEFECT OBSERVATIONS (BACK ONLY)
=========================================

**Cognitive reasoning procedure:**

For each region (4 corners + 4 edges on BACK):

1. **Zoom mentally into region and describe shape, color, and reflectivity.**
2. **Ask a question: "Is what I see consistent with printing or damage?"**
3. **If damage:** describe material disruption (fiber exposure, color break, texture distortion).
4. **If uncertain:** describe both hypotheses and select confidence = "medium" or "low".

**Interpretive rules for tricky detections:**

**Crease vs. Glare:**
- Crease = consistent linear deformation following substrate contour; glare = optical highlight that shifts with geometry.

**Dent:**
- Local shadow bowl without color break.

**Fray or Whitening:**
- High-contrast edge where core paper color differs from printed ink.

**Rough-cut factory edge:**
- Perfectly linear fiber texture; do not treat as damage.

**DEFECT TYPES:**

**CORNER DEFECTS**
- **corner_whitening**: White paper core visible at tip (check every corner!)
- **corner_chip**: Missing piece at corner tip
- **corner_fray**: Separated layers at corner
- **corner_bend**: Corner folded/bent from plane
- **corner_crease**: Visible crease line through corner
- **corner_pristine**: Corner is perfectly sharp with no wear (used for mandatory analysis)

**EDGE DEFECTS**
- **edge_wear**: Whitening along edge from handling
- **edge_chip**: Small missing piece from edge
- **edge_nick**: Small indentation in edge
- **rough_cut**: Factory cutting imperfection (linear)
- **edge_pristine**: Edge is perfectly clean with no wear (used for mandatory analysis)

**SURFACE DEFECTS**
- **surface_scratch**: Linear mark on surface (distinguish from glare!)
- **surface_scuff**: Dulled area from rubbing
- **surface_stain**: Discoloration or spot
- **surface_dent**: Indentation (look for shadows)
- **surface_wrinkle**: Ripple or wave in surface

**PRINT DEFECTS**
- **print_speck**: Tiny dot or spot from printing
- **print_line**: Thin line (roller line, ink streak)
- **print_misregistration**: Colors slightly offset
- **print_dot_loss**: Missing ink dots
- **color_fade**: Color appears lighter than expected

**Category**: Assign to corner|edge|surface|print|structural

**Location**: Use this format:
- Corners: back_top_left_corner, back_top_right_corner, back_bottom_left_corner, back_bottom_right_corner
