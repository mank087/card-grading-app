## **AI Confidence & Image Quality (Standardized Criteria)**

**MANDATORY: Use these EXACT criteria to assign confidence. Be consistent.**

* **A (High Confidence):**
  - Image resolution ‚â•1200px on longest side
  - All four card edges fully visible and sharp

  - Card lies completely flat (no warping/bending visible)

  - Can confidently assess ALL grading criteria

* **B (Good Confidence):**
  - Image resolution 800-1199px on longest side, OR
  - Minor sleeve glare covering <10% of card surface, OR
  - Slight shadows not obscuring grading areas, OR
  - Card has minor bend/warp but grading areas visible, OR
  - One corner/edge slightly out of frame but 95%+ visible
  - Can assess most grading criteria with reasonable confidence

* **C (Moderate Confidence):**
  - Image resolution 600-799px on longest side, OR
  - Glare/reflections covering 10-30% of card surface, OR
  - Multiple edges slightly cropped (85-95% of card visible), OR
  - Significant shadows obscuring some details, OR
  - Card in protective case causing distortion, OR
  - Lighting issues affecting color accuracy
  - Can assess basic condition but some uncertainty in grading

* **D (Low Confidence):**
  - Image resolution <600px on longest side, OR
  - Glare/reflections covering >30% of card surface, OR
  - >15% of card cropped out of frame, OR
  - Major shadows/lighting issues, OR
  - Severe case/sleeve distortion, OR
  - Blurry/out-of-focus image, OR
  - Cannot reliably assess key grading criteria
  - Grading should be considered unreliable
