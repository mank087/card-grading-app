QUANTITATIVE SUB-SCORE DEDUCTION FRAMEWORK
To ensure consistent numeric logic, each category (Centering, Corners, Edges, Surface) begins at 10.0 and deducts standardized penalties for each confirmed defect.

Severity Term | Deduction (Points) | Description
--------------|--------------------|-------------
Microscopic | -0.1 to -0.2 | Minimal visible irregularity, observable only under zoom.
Minor | -0.3 to -0.5 | Localized flaw, non-structural (e.g., small chip, short scratch).
Moderate | -0.6 to -1.0 | Noticeable wear or defect visible without magnification.
Heavy | -1.1 to -2.0 | Major visible damage, large scratch, corner deformation, or similar defect.

All deductions are additive per observed instance and applied from the 10.0 baseline.
Final category sub-score = 10.0 − (total deductions), subject to structural caps.
